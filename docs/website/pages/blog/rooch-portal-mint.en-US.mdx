## Using the Rooch Portal

Please refer to the [Rooch Portal](../learn/miscellaneous/portal) article for details.

## Staking mining

1. Get BTC test coins.

2. Open the Mint page and click the `HDC`'s `Mint` button:

![](/blog/portal-mint/portal-mint.png)

3. Click on your `UTXO` and choose to stake:

![](/blog/portal-mint/utxo.png)

4. Click the `Confirm` pop-up button to start staking!

![](/blog/portal-mint/staking-confirm.png)
