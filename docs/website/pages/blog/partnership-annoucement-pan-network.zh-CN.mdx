Rooch Network is thrilled to partner with **PAN Network**, a trailblazing project that is transforming Bitcoin payments by enhancing the scalability, security, and usability of the Lightning Network. Together, we are launching a **Dual Mining Program** on the **Grow Bitcoin Platform**, offering participants the chance to earn **$GROW**, **$xPAN points**, and unlock exclusive rewards. Whether you’re a miner, NFT collector, or a Bitcoin enthusiast, this is your chance to immerse yourself in two groundbreaking ecosystems while earning valuable rewards!

### How to Participate:

1. Visit **Rooch Network's [Grow Bitcoin Platform](https://grow.rooch.network/)** to begin.
2. Earn **$GROW** by engaging with activities on the Grow Bitcoin platform.
3. Go to the **PAN Network** page and use your **$GROW** tokens to vote for PAN Network.
4. The event concludes at **6 PM, February 24th (UTC+8)**.

### Event Highlights:

- **Dual Mining Rewards**: Earn both **$xPAN points** from PAN Network and exclusive Rooch rewards.
    - **$xPAN Points** are the cornerstone for unlocking valuable rewards from PAN Network.
    - After the Token Generation Event (TGE), **$xPAN points** will be airdropped as **$PAN tokens**, significantly increasing their value.
    - Top participants will earn generous **$xPAN** rewards. Climb the leaderboard and secure your spot among the top miners!

### Reward Distribution:

**Leaderboard Rewards**:

| **Ranking**    | **Individual Rewards** |
|----------------|------------------------|
| **1 - 100**    | 80 xPAN each           |
| **101 - 500**  | 40 xPAN each           |
| **501 - 1000** | 20 xPAN each           |

### Event Rules:

1. **How to Participate:**
    - Complete the voting task on the event page to qualify for participation.
2. **Reward Distribution:**
    - Detailed information about reward distribution will be provided to the Rooch community after the event ends.

### Why Join the Dual Mining Program?

This isn’t just another rewards program—it’s an exciting opportunity to be part of an evolving ecosystem at the crossroads of **Bitcoin** and **Bitcoin Payments**. By participating, you not only earn exclusive rewards but also contribute to the expansion of the **Rooch Network** and **PAN Network**, playing a pivotal role in the future of decentralized finance and Bitcoin adoption.
