**Before we announce the new milestone of Rooch Network, we are eager to invite our community to join us in discussing the future prospects of the BTC and Rooch ecosystems. To this end, we are launching an article contest open to everyone. Participants will have the opportunity to share a prize pool of 240,000 $ROOCH.** This is a valuable chance for our community members to earn $ROOCH in the early stage.  Now let's take a look at the details of this event below.

### **Step 1:**

First, repost and like Rooch's article contest [event post](https://x.com/RoochNetwork/status/1796112069214847096). Then, write an article or a long tweet based on the following themes. We look forward to your unique insights and in-depth analysis as we explore the future prospects of the BTC and Rooch ecosystems together:

- **The Development Trends and Application Prospects of the Bitcoin Ecosystem**
- **Discussion of Rooch's Layer2 Technology and Comparison with Other Layer2 Technologies**

### **Step 2:**

**Share your article on Twitter, mention @RoochNetwork and 3 KOLs, and use the hashtags #RoochNetwork and #BitcoinEcosystem.** Encourage your social media followers to comment, like, and share the post for better exposure. 

---

### **Awards:**

After the event ends, we will select **20 high-quality posts** from those submitted on this topic. **All eligible posts must have at least 20 interactions (likes, shares, or comments) and at least 400 characters.**

**Rooch officials will select the winners based on multiple dimensions such as the content and views of the articles/long tweets.**

- **First Prize**: 2 winners, each receiving 30,000 $ROOCH
- **Second Prize**: 4 winners, each receiving 20,000 $ROOCH
- **Third Prize**: 6 winners, each receiving 10,000 $ROOCH
- **Participation Prize**: 8 winners, each receiving $5,000 ROOCH

A total of 240,000 $ROOCH will be awarded.

**All content must be original and must be published by 16:00 (UTC+8) on June 14, 2024 to be eligible for the rewards.**

---

### **Rules and Terms**

- Only posts and articles with at least 20 interactions (total number of emojis, comments, or shares) using the hashtags #RoochNetwork and #BitcoinEcosystem during the event period will qualify as eligible content.
- Any misuse of the hashtags will result in disqualification from the event.
- Only original, new content is eligible. Previously submitted works are not eligible.
- You may post multiple high-quality pieces of content to increase your chances of winning.
- **Prizes will be distributed to your account during the $ROOCH TGE (Token Generation Event).**
