We are thrilled to announce a strategic partnership between Rooch Network and Yescoin, one of the largest and fastest-growing mini-apps on Telegram, to introduce the **Dual Mining Program** on the Grow Bitcoin Platform! This collaboration enables you to earn generous rewards from both Yescoin and Rooch Network. Don’t miss this opportunity to maximize your benefits—completely free of cost!

### How to Participate:

1. Connect to Rooch Network's [Grow Bitcoin Platform](https://grow.rooch.network/).
2. Earn $GROW by participating on the Grow Bitcoin platform.
3. Navigate to the Yescoin page on the platform and vote for Yescoin using your $GROW tokens.
4. End time:  8pm, January 27th(UTC+8） 

### Event Highlights:

- **Dual Reward Mechanism**: **Earn both YESCOIN points** and **exclusive Rooch platform rewards**.
    - The more **$GROW** you spend on voting, the higher your ranking! The top 2,000 addresses will split  77,250,000 YESCOIN points.
    - YESCOIN is the incentive points within the Yescoin ecosystem, awarded for Proof of Work (POW) activities on the platform. These points can be used to participate in future Yescoin **airdrops**.
    - All participants will also earn **$GROW** and **BitXP** from Rooch Network, which are tied to future Rooch airdrops.

### Why Participate?

This is a unique opportunity to earn dual rewards from two innovative Web3 ecosystems, enhancing the value of your digital assets. By joining the Dual Mining Program, you’re not just earning rewards, but also contributing to the growth of both Yescoin and Rooch Networks.

**Don’t miss out on this incredible opportunity!** Join the Dual Mining Program today and help shape the future of Web3 with Rooch and Yescoin!
